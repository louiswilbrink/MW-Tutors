About Us

The effectiveness of our curriculum comes from joining a professional ability to communicate in English with media/communication technologies such as Skype, Googledocs, and our website. The student can visit our website and review collection of news content we have uploaded daily. They can also visit the Calendar page to see what times are available for online tutoring and schedule a lesson at their convenience.
The lessons are held through a collaborative workspace consisting of Skype, Googledocs, and the website. Communicating through Skype, the tutor and student can read news articles together, view news clips with transcripts provided, use the topics of the news to open up dialogue, and even share screens to provide technical instructions. Our business also uses googledocs.com to share resources such as grammar guides and study materials.
